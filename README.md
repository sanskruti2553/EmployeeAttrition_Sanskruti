# Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on understanding employee attrition using an HR dataset. The goal is to identify the factors that may influence employees to leave the company and provide useful insights that can help improve employee retention.

The project includes data cleaning, data exploration, visualizations, and a comparison of different prediction methods. The results can help organizations better understand employee behavior and make informed HR decisions.

---

## Dataset

* **Dataset:** HR_Attrition.csv
* **Total Employees:** 1,470
* **Target:** Employee Attrition (Yes/No)

The dataset contains employee information such as:

* Age
* Gender
* Department
* Job Role
* Monthly Income
* Distance from Home
* Years at Company
* Overtime
* Job Satisfaction
* Work Experience
* And other employee-related details.

## Steps Performed

1. Loaded and explored the dataset.
2. Checked and cleaned the data.
3. Studied employee information using charts and graphs.
4. Compared different prediction approaches.
5. Identified the important factors related to employee attrition.
6. Summarized the findings in simple language.

---

## Key Findings

* Around **16%** of employees left the company.
* Overtime, monthly income, work experience, years at the company, and distance from home were some of the factors related to employee attrition.
* Employee attrition is influenced by several factors rather than a single reason.
* One prediction approach provided better results than the others.
